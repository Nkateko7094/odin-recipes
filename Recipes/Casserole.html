Casserole Recipe

Description: This ham and hash brown casserole is quick and easy to make, not to mention delicious!

Ingredients:

  • cooking spray
  • 1 (32 ounce) package frozen hash brown potatoes
  • 2 (10.5 ounce) cans condensed cream of potato soup
  • 1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
  • 2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 8 ounces cooked, diced ham
  • 1 Â½ cups grated Parmesan cheese'

    Step 1:

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.

    2. Step 2:

  2. Mix together hash browns, condensed soup, sour cream, Cheddar cheese, and ham in a bowl until well combined.

    3. Step 3:

  3. Spread evenly into the prepared dish. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.

    4. Step 4:

  4. Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly and lightly brown, about 1 hour.

    5. Step 5:

  5. Enjoy!
